About Us

Since being founded back in 2008, Manage My Bookkeeping has helped many small businesses, including plumbers, electricians, car repairers and more.

We utilise cloud technologies like Xero, the G-Suite and Dropbox to speed up your business' efficiency, so you can do more in less time.

We pride ourselves on being legislation compliant and competent, including:

  • Tax Practitioners Board as a registered BAS Agent
  • ATO BAS Agent portal for secure online lodgement
  • Xero Silver Certified Advisors
  • Quickbooks Certified Advisors
  • Hubdoc Certified Advanced Partner
  • Up-to-date insurance

Kylie Shea

I began working initially with a family-owned company who needed help managing the administration side of their fast-growing business. Although I was brought on to look after 'the books', this quickly led me to find other gaps missing and ways to help. My services grew to include sending emails, making phone calls, administration, banking help and generally running their business on a daily basis so they could concentrate on sales and building bigger.

Now I thrive on helping small businesses to grow - helping business owners build their business by relieving them of the paperwork and help them to setup systems and processes specifically for their business and to let them get back to what they do best.
