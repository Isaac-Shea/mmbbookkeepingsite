ABOUT US

Manage My Bookkeeping was founded back in 2008 and has helped many small businesses, including:

  • Plumbers
  • Electricians
  • Roofing Contractors
  • Transport Operators
  • Solar Retailers
  • IT Professionals
  • Concretors
  • Car Repairers, and;
  • Other small businesses

We utilise cloud technology including:

  • Online accounting with Xero and Quickbooks to speed up and automate your systems
  • Online solutions for email including the GSuite
  • File and data sharing through Dropbox, TeamViewer, etc.

We provide administration and bookkeeping services for your business.
We keep everything simple and easy for you whilst still complying with all relevant legislation.
We are constantly embracing new technologies to save you both time and money.
We work with you, and can tailor a solution from helping out with administration and bookkeeping work, to running a complete virtual office.
Let us help lower your stress levels - we can get you sorted to save you time, keep you compliant, keep you on top of your bills, and keep your customers paying you!

We pride ourselves on being compliant, including:

  • Tax Pracitioners Board as a registered BAS Agent
  • ATO BAS Agent portal for secure online lodgement
  • Xero Silver Certified Advisors
  • Quickbooks Certified Advisors
  • Hubdoc Certified Advanced Partner
  • Up-to-date Insurance

Kylie Shea

I began working initially with a family-owned company who needed help managing the administration side of their fast-growing business. Although brought on to look after "the books" this quickly led me to find other gaps missing, and ways to help. My services grew to include emails, phone calls, administration, banking help, and generally running their business on a daily basis so they could concentrate on sales and building bigger.

Now I thrive on helping small businesses to grow - helping business owners to build their business by relieving them of the paperwork, and help them to set up systems and processes specifically for their business, and to let them get back to what they do best.

I am the first to admit my OCD-like traits, and my need for organisation with t's crossed and i's dotted. Just ask my husband and kids! I love putting these skills to use - there's only so much I can use my Dymo labeller on at my place. And now with the huge advances in technology I have ways to organise your business online to make finding that document or processing that purchase much easier.
